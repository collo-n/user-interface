Gestalt Principles

Gestalt is a psychology term which means "unified whole". It refers to theories of visual perception developed by German psychologists in the 1920s.
Similarity occurs when object look similar to one another eople often perceive them as group or pattern.
Continuation occurs when the eye is compelled to move through one object and continue to another object.
Closure occurs when an object is incomplete. If enough of the shape is indicated, people perceive the whole by filling in the missing information.
